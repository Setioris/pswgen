Password Strength Tips

  • Length matters most: Aim for at least 12 characters.
  • Variety is key: Use a mix of character types (upper, lower, numbers, symbols).
  • Avoid patterns: Don't use sequences like "1234" or "qwerty".
  • Don't reuse: Each account should have a unique password.
  • Consider a manager: Password managers can generate and store strong passwords securely.
